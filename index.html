Collection of benchmarks useful for scientists evaluating the capabilities of frontier models across open-weight and private systems.

Fossils preserve the imprint of vanished worlds; benchmarks do something similar for language models, pressing on their weights until hidden structure becomes visible through what they can recover, connect, and reason about.

The premise is simple: gather helpful evals for understanding the capabilities of models across a broad swath of science benchmarks, then make the material easy to compare, annotate, and revisit.

Rather than flatten science evaluation into a single scoreboard, the site should show what each benchmark actually probes: retrieval, multi-step reasoning, structured extraction, and the problems where frontier models still have real room to improve.
